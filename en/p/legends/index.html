Project Ends, Legacy Remains.

These projects are no longer maintain. All these thoughts and discussions form a positive power to motivate the world. Even though they stopped here, these projects are still worth to see and learn.

OCF Lab

OCF Lab is an information platform focused on the issues of open technology, including open source, open data, open government and digital rights and internet freedom. OCF Lab intends to make these issues more close to the public and bring the positive force of the technology to the society.

The Openists

The "Openists" is a project that gathers people who are interested in open-source or have are open-minded. We provide a series of open source related workshops, gives stages for people who want to share their knowledge and professional skills to the world.

PTT ID Profiling

PTT is currently one of the most influential social media platform in Taiwan. PTT is now facing the same problem: growing amount of disinformation, media manipulation, fake IDs and cyber army operators, just like other social medias. We are creating a suite of open source tools, open data, and open licensed analysing reports, aim to find out the abnormal behavior from virtual IDs, and you can analysis these original data if you don't believe what you saw.
